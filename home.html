Welcome to Totally Dental!

At Totally Dental, our patients are the most important part of our practice. We cater our treatment to each patient's needs and desires while promoting their overall dental health. We welcome patients of any age into our dental family.

Meet Dr. Josie Keat

Dr. Josie Keat has been providing dental services to the Northern Virginia community since graduating from Georgetown University School of Dentistry in 1981. She took a brief hiatus as her husband’s diplomatic career took them to Asia and South America where they had their two sons. But in 2001, they came back home to Northern Virginia where Dr. Keat resumed practicing dentistry. Growing her dental family and helping them stay healthy is the chief mission of her life. Dr. Keat has met many wonderful people along the way and she can’t wait to meet you!
